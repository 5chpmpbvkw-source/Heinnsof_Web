THE CHALLENGE

Energy Strategy at the Board Level

Institutional investors, infrastructure funds, and large industrial operators face energy access decisions that can determine the viability of entire portfolios. Whether underwriting a new acquisition, structuring an infrastructure investment, or setting a multi-decade energy strategy, the decisions are high-stakes and deeply technical. Yet most boards and investment committees lack the specialist expertise to interrogate these questions rigorously.

Heinnsof bridges that gap — bringing independent, technically grounded advisory to the most consequential energy decisions our clients face, without the conflicts of interest that compromise infrastructure brokers or equipment vendors.

How We Advise Institutional Clients

01
Board & Investment Committee Advisory
We provide independent, technically grounded energy access briefings for boards and investment committees — translating complex grid, regulatory, and infrastructure risk into language that supports confident decision-making at the highest level.
02
Portfolio Energy Strategy
For funds and operators with multi-site infrastructure portfolios, we develop cohesive energy access strategies that identify portfolio-wide opportunities, rationalise exposure, and align power planning with investment horizons.
03
Infrastructure Risk & Opportunity Assessment
We assess energy infrastructure risk in acquisition and development scenarios — providing the technical and commercial evidence base that supports underwriting, due diligence, and financial modelling decisions.

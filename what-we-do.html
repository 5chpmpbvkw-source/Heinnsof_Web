Grid Connection

Grid Connection at Scale

The global race for grid capacity is intensifying. From hyperscale data centres requiring hundreds of megawatts to green hydrogen facilities with transformational load profiles, the demand for grid access now far outstrips what conventional processes can deliver.

Heinnsof's grid connection practice accelerates every stage of the connection process — from pre-application strategy through offer acceptance and construction oversight — applying deep technical and regulatory expertise to eliminate delays and protect project timelines.

Advisory That Delivers

Heinnsof's advisory model is built on a single principle: every engagement must produce an actionable outcome. We do not deliver reports that gather dust. We structure engagements around milestones — from initial feasibility through financial close and construction delivery.

Sustainability

Sustainable Energy Solutions

The transition to net-zero requires more than ambition — it requires a credible roadmap aligned to operational realities. Heinnsof works with energy-intensive organisations to design carbon strategies that are technically sound, commercially viable, and reportable under international frameworks.

From Scope 2 reduction through renewable procurement to full decarbonisation pathways, our sustainability advisory practice supports clients at every stage of the transition journey.
